Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on: 20-03-2025 18:55:12
Policy Details

JANARTHANAN SUBANANTHAM believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore a liberal cancellation policy. Under this policy:

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made immediately after placing the order. Requests may not be entertained if the order has already been communicated to vendors/merchants or shipping has begun.
  • No cancellation requests are accepted for perishable items like flowers or eatables. Refund/replacement may be provided if the quality of the product is not satisfactory.
  • Damaged or defective items must be reported to Customer Service within 7 days of receipt. The request will be verified with the merchant before action is taken.
  • If the product received is not as shown or does not meet expectations, report to Customer Service within 7 days. Appropriate action will be taken after review.
  • For products with manufacturer warranties, complaints should be referred to the manufacturer. Refunds approved by JANARTHANAN SUBANANTHAM will take 6-8 days to process.
